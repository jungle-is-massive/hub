Should Jungle join the Advertising Association?

Two membership options, different value propositions. What each costs, what each unlocks, and how they fit our growth strategy.

Bundle cost

+ VAT · both memberships

This is not an intermediary play. The AA will not send Jungle pitches or put us in agency consideration sets. That distinction matters for how we evaluate it.

What it does is give Jungle structured access to rooms we don't currently get into — senior brand leaders, C-suite agency peers, Government receptions, and curated international moments. The value is relationship infrastructure and thought leadership platform, both of which run parallel to intermediary engagement rather than replacing it.

What this is, and what it isn't

The AA is not an intermediary. Joining does not put Jungle in pitch consideration sets and should not be evaluated against that metric. There are no shortlists, no direct briefs, no lead generation.

The value is relationship infrastructure — consistent access to senior people at the right moments, a credible context for Jungle to show up as a thought leader rather than a vendor, and a platform to build visibility with the kinds of brand and agency leaders who eventually influence or make agency selection decisions. That's a slower burn, and it compounds over time.

It runs in parallel to intermediary engagement. The question is whether £4k–8k/year for that access is the right allocation at this stage of Jungle's growth trajectory.
