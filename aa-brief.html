04 / Reality checks — don't ignore these
⚠
This is not a passive investment
You'll be expected to contribute ideas, show up prepared, bring valuable guests, and engage actively. If you don't, you'll get very little back. A membership that sits on a shelf is wasted money at any price.
⚠
The real cost is significantly higher than £4,500
Membership is the entry point — not the total. Add event participation fees, Cannes travel and accommodation (easily £15–25k for one person for the week), time investment across multiple events per year. Build a realistic budget before committing.
⚠
Only valuable if aligned to Jungle's actual direction
The AA asked the right question: are you trying to build Jungle in the UK first, or globally? This membership accelerates international reach. If the priority is UK pipeline, there are better-targeted ways to spend this money. Get the strategic question right first.
The decision hinges on one question. "Are we building Jungle in the UK first — or globally?"
If the answer is UK first
Consider the membership, skip the events
The intelligence layer, peer positioning, and UK network value still justify £4,500. Hold off on Cannes and international travel until pipeline warrants it. Test before scaling.
If the answer is global
Go all in — but plan the budget properly
The international expansion play is the biggest lever here. Saudi, Nigeria, US market access via structured trade missions is genuinely hard to replicate independently. But budget the full cost, not just membership.
05 / Bottom line
The verdict

Not a "join and get leads" play.

This is a network accelerator, a reputation builder, and an international expansion platform. The £4,500 membership fee is almost beside the point — the question is whether Jungle is ready to treat this like a system, not a one-off activity.

Worth it if: you're serious about international growth
Worth it if: you have a POV you're ready to put in rooms
Worth it if: the SMI narrative needs global amplification
Worth it if: someone owns the relationship actively

Next decision to pressure-test: where Jungle actually needs growth right now (UK vs global) · what role this plays vs intermediaries · whether Cannes alone is worth it vs the full AA programme. That's the real conversation.
